▌ FIELD OBSERVATIONS · CLEARED FOR BRIEFING
These undead beings resemble intelligent zombies. Some cults keep them in temples as guardians. Like zombies they must be dismembered to stop them moving. Many have no bandages and move quite quickly. A mummy has twice the Strength of the person it once was, 1.5× Constitution, and two thirds of original Dexterity. Preserved with bitumen and bandages, they are especially vulnerable to fire — normal damage, but much harder to extinguish.
