True LoyaltyThorne sold the Vane Grimoire's twin volume to Mother Vane eleven years ago, before he understood what it was. His guilt over this drives everything he does now.

TwistIf the investigators show him page 34 of the Grimoire, he recognizes his own marginalia — he has read further into it than he admits.
