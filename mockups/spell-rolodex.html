Mythos Spell
Dread Curse of Azathoth
a.k.a. Utterance of the Last Syllable · Pitiful Draining
◈ 4 Magic Points ☾ 1d6 SAN ⏱ 1 round
Effect

The sorcerer draws on the energy of the Outer God to drain POWER directly from the victim. The caster must win an opposed POW roll against the target; on success the victim loses 3d6 POW. Knowing the spell implies knowledge of the secret Last Syllable, which grants respect and fear among those versed in the Mythos.

Known by
Mother Vane Obed Osgood The Whisperer
👁 Keeper Note
Casting in front of investigators is itself a 1/1d6 SAN sighting. If the party recovers the Fenwick fragment, they can learn the counter-syllable.
