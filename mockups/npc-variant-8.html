Voice & Manner

Reedy, precise diction — clears throat before disagreeing. Slight Boston Brahmin affect. Never raises his voice, even when alarmed.

Quirk

Compulsively straightens books that aren't level. Keeps a magnifying loupe on a neck chain, fidgets with it when lying.

Motivation

Wants the Whitlock name restored after his brother's disappearance discredited the shop. Will trade rare texts for information about the Kingsport incident.

Secret Notes

Owns a copy of the Fenwick Glossary he's hidden behind a false shelf panel, aware it's cursed but too curious to burn it. His brother didn't vanish — he sold him passage to the Y'ha-nthlei cult in exchange for the shop's debts being forgiven. Will panic and confess if pressed on the topic of Innsmouth gold.
