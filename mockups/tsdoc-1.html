▍ PUBLIC BRIEFING · PLAYERS MAY VIEW

These undead beings resemble intelligent zombies. Some cults keep mummies in temples to serve as guardians. Like zombies, they must be dismembered to stop them moving. Contrary to popular belief, many mummies have no bandages and can move quite quickly. A mummy has twice the Strength of the living person it once was, 1.5× the Constitution, and two thirds of the original Dexterity. Because Egyptian mummies were preserved with bitumen and bandages, fire is especially effective — it deals normal damage but is much harder to extinguish.
