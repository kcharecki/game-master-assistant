GM Secret Notes

Obadiah is already three-quarters Deep One in physiology — remove his hat and coat only for the final reveal. He genuinely fears Lord Blackthorn's trade fleet uncovering the refinery's ledgers, since Blackthorn's audit would expose the tithe payments as bodies, not cargo. If Selina is confronted directly, Obadiah will offer the party a "tithe exemption" in exchange for silence — a trap: the exemption is temporary and applies to them alone.
