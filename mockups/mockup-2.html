❖ The Sunken City of Vael'Tharion

Long before the Sundering, Vael'Tharion stood as the jewel of the Aldermoor Coast — a city of pale marble spires raised by the Tideborn who bargained with the deep gods for dominion over the waves. Its archmage, Erevan the Drowned, sealed a fragment of the Star That Fell beneath the cathedral, believing he could bend its radiance to eternal day. When the wards failed in the year 612 A.S., the sea swallowed the city whole in a single tolling of the bells. Sailors still claim the spires sing on moonless nights, calling the unwary down to Erevan's court.
